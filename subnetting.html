📊 Subnetting – Sou-Rezo

Aprann kalkile sou-rezo an Kreyòl ak egzanp klè, tablo, ak kesyon pratik.

Poukisa Subnetting Itil?

Subnetting se pwosesis divize yon gwo rezo IP an plizyè ti sou-rezo. Sa pèmèt:

🏢

Òganizasyon

Separe rezo biwo diferan: Depatman Finansye, IT, RH — chak nan sou-rezo pa yo.

🛡️

Sekirite

Limite kominikasyon ant depatman yo pou redui enpak si yon depatman konpwomèt.

⚡

Pèfòmans

Redui trafik broadcast — broadcast pa vwayaje ant sou-rezo diferan.

Konsèp Fondamantal yo

🔢

Network ID (Rezo)

Premye adrès nan sou-rezo a. Reprezante rezo a antye. Pa ka asiye bay yon òs.

Egzanp: 192.168.1.0/24

📢

Broadcast Address

Dènye adrès nan sou-rezo a. Pakè voye bay tout aparèy nan sou-rezo a an menm tan. Pa ka asiye bay yon òs.

Egzanp: 192.168.1.255

💻

Usable Host Range

Tout adrès ant Network ID ak Broadcast. Sa ou ka asiye bay òdinatè, sèvè, routeur, ak lòt aparèy.

🔢

CIDR Notation

Pou ekri adrès rezo avèk masque li: 192.168.1.0/24 vle di masque a gen 24 bit "1" konsekitif.

📐
Fòmil Fondamantal:

Kantite Òs Itilizabl = 2ⁿ − 2 kote n = kantite bit ki rezève pou pati Òs la. Nou retire 2 pou Network ID ak Broadcast.

Egzanp Detaye — Kalkile yon Sou-Rezo /26

🎯 Pwoblèm

Ou gen rezo 192.168.10.0/24. Ou bezwen divize li an 4 sou-rezo egal pou 4 depatman ki chak bezwen ~60 aparèy. Ki CIDR pou itilize?

1

Detèmine Bit Nesesè

62 òs útilizabl = /26 (2⁶ − 2 = 62). Donk nou bezwen 6 bit pou pati Òs la.

2

Kalkile Masque

/26 = 32 − 6 = 26 bit pou pati rezo. Masque: 255.255.255.192 (192 = 11000000 binè)

3

Idantifye Entèval (Block Size)

256 − 192 = 64. Chak sou-rezo avanse pa 64.

4

Kalkile Chak Sou-Rezo

Sou-rezo #1: .0, Sou-rezo #2: .64, Sou-rezo #3: .128, Sou-rezo #4: .192

Egzanp /27 — Kalkile Sou-Rezo 8 yo

# Rezo: 192.168.1.0/27 (Masque: 255.255.255.224)
# Block Size: 256 − 224 = 32
# Chak sou-rezo gen 30 Òs itilizabl (2⁵ − 2)

Kesyon Pratik — Eseye Tèt Ou!

Repons: /27 = 5 bit pou òs. 2⁵ − 2 = 32 − 2 = 30 òs itilizabl. Masque: 255.255.255.224.

Repons: /26 gen block size 64. 64 + 64 − 1 = 10.0.1.127 se broadcast la.

Repons: /24 = Network ID 172.16.4.0, Broadcast 172.16.4.255. Adrès 172.16.4.200 ap nan plag .1 à .254 — li nan sou-rezo 172.16.4.0/24.

Repons: /26 bay 62 òs (sifi). /27 bay sèlman 30 (pa sifi). Repons: /26.

Repons: /28 = 28 bit "1". Dènye okté: 11110000 = 240. Donk masque: 255.255.255.240. Òs itilizabl: 2⁴−2 = 14.
